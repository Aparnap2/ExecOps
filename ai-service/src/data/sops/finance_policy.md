# Finance Policy

## Purpose
This policy defines rules for financial transactions and approvals.

## General Rules

### Rule 1: Expense Approval Threshold

## Trigger
New expense is submitted

## Condition
- Expense amount exceeds $500
- No manager approval attached

## Action
- Route to manager for approval
- Notify finance team of pending approval

---

### Rule 2: Vendor Payment Validation

## Trigger
Vendor invoice is received

## Condition
- Invoice amount exceeds $10,000
- No purchase order number referenced
- Vendor is not in approved vendor list

## Action
- Block payment processing
- Request additional documentation
- Escalate to finance manager

---

### Rule 3: Budget Alert

## Trigger
Monthly spending report is generated

## Condition
- Department has exceeded 90% of monthly budget
- No variance explanation provided

## Action
- Alert department head
- Request budget reallocation plan
- Freeze non-essential spending
